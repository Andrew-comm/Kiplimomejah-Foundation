What We Do & Why We Do

At kiplimomejah Foundation , we are committed to providing aid and support to those affected by floods in Kenya. Our mission is to alleviate the suffering of flood victims by providing essential supplies such as food, clean water, shelter, and medical assistance. We understand the devastating impact that floods can have on communities, and we strive to offer assistance to those in need during these challenging times.

Through our fundraising efforts and the generous support of our donors, we are able to make a meaningful difference in the lives of flood-affected individuals and families. Whether it's distributing emergency relief kits or assisting with long-term recovery efforts, we are dedicated to providing assistance and hope to those affected by floods in Kenya.
